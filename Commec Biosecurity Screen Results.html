Benchmarking Aegis against

, a sequence-based biosecurity screening tool

IBBIS: International Biosecurity and Biosafety Initiative for Science
Methods
  • Cholix toxin sequences redesigned using ProteinMPNN at two sampling temperatures: T=0.3 and T=1.5
  • mutconserved — mutations at conserved catalytic residues; expected benign
  • mutoutside — mutations outside the binding site; expected toxic
  • 30 sequences per class per temperature (120 total) went through DNA obfuscation, where DNA fragments were randomly scrambled to further diverge from the wild-type sequence
  • Sequences screened with COMMEC v1.0.0 biorisk step only (taxonomy steps skipped); sequences matching known virulence factors in the IBBIS biorisk HMM database flagged as Warning
